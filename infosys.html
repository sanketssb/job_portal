Infosys Recruitment 2022:


Company Name Infosys
Post Name Graphic designer
Salary ₹3.58 LPA*
Experience 0-2 yr

Job Description:

Infosys Off-campus hiring candidates for the post of Graphic designer.

Responsibilities:

  • Design and create high-quality visual design for online training (presentation templates, illustrations, animations) and UX design (prototypes, design systems, style guides)
  • Collaborate with faculty to understand the content and come up with ways to tell the story in a visually interesting way.
  • Create marketing communication material for digital media to promote training programs.
  • Design icons and create a visual language.
  • Create visual design and motion graphics for video.

    • Eligibility Criteria:

  • Bachelor’s degree preferably in Graphic Design or a related discipline with at least one year of work experience in Graphic Design.
  • Must have a portfolio which demonstrates academic and professional work in Graphic Design.

    • Preferred skill:

  • Art and Design skills
  • Proficiency in using software like Photoshop, Illustrator and Sketch or equivalent.
  • Must have good visualization skills, and a flair for creative thinking.
  • Keen eye for creativity and attention to detail. Soft skills
  • Self-motivated, able to prioritize and manage workload and meet critical project deadlines.
  • Strong communication skills.
  • Be proactive and have the drive and ability to handle projects in individual capacity as well as while being a part of a global team.
  • Should have the ability to work in a fast-paced environment and to successfully juggle multiple projects simultaneously.

About Infosys:

Infosys Limited is an Indian multinational information technology company that provides business consulting, information technology, and outsourcing services. The company was founded in Pune and is headquartered in Bangalore
